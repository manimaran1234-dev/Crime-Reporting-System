❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a crime anonymously?
Select "Yes" for anonymous reporting when submitting your report. Your personal information won't be shared.
Can I edit my report after submission?
Reports cannot be edited once submitted for security reasons. Contact support if you need to add important information.
How long does it take to get a response?
High priority cases receive immediate attention. Medium priority cases are typically addressed within 24 hours.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted end-to-end and stored securely. We never share personal information without consent.
